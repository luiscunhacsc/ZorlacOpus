# Fortress of Zorlac

A modern, neon-cyberpunk reimagining of the classic ZX81 game *Fortress of Zorlac*, brought to 2026 standards with vibrant visuals, engaging sound effects, and smooth gameplay. 

## About the Game

The evil Zorlac has built heavily defended, rotating fortresses that are slowly advancing towards your position. Your mission is to destroy the rotating walls to expose Zorlac's vulnerable core, while avoiding the indestructible cannons that fire back at you.

## Features

*   **Neon-Cyberpunk Aesthetics**: Experience the classic gameplay with a fresh, 2026 coat of paint featuring glowing visuals and particle effects.
*   **Intense Action**: Blast holes in the rotating defenses and dodge enemy fire.
*   **Progressive Difficulty**: Destroy the core to advance to the next level. The fortress gets tougher as you progress!
*   **Local High Scores**: Compete for the top spot on the leaderboard with a local save system.

## Controls

*   **Movement**: Use `W A S D` or `Arrow Keys` to move your ship.
*   **Shoot**: Press `SPACE` or `Left Click` to fire your weapon.

## How to Play

1.  **Initialize Attack**: Click the start button on the main menu to begin your mission.
2.  **Breach the Defenses**: Shoot the rotating walls of the fortress to create an opening.
3.  **Destroy the Core**: Once you have a clear shot, fire at the central core (Zorlac) to destroy it and advance to the next level.
4.  **Survive**: You have 5 lives. Avoid getting hit by the enemy's indestructible cannons or touching the fortress walls. If the advancing fortress reaches too close to your ship, it's game over!

## Setup

Simply open `index.html` in your modern web browser (Chrome, Edge, etc.) to start playing instantly. No installation or compilation required.
